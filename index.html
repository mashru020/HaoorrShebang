Owing to this fact, this gorgeous place is more than ideal for hosting this awe-inspiring project which is set to become the first of it's kind.

Keeping in mind the ever-adventurous and enthusiastic travelers and tourists of Bangladesh, we are the first to envisage building a hotel to open the door fortourists and travelers to reside in this vicinity and enjoy the mind bogglingly beautiful scenery of the Meghalaya Mountains and Tanguar Haor. Interestingly enough, there is a Land Customs Station in Charagaon through which different types of materials get imported from India, and the project stands right beside this Land Customs Station. Besides, mesmerizing tourist attractions like Niladri Lake and Bareker Tila can be very easily visited from our project site. Details of theafor ementioned places are given on our home page.

This project is all about creating a state-of-the-art and international standard hotel. This hotel will be created by expert and dignified engineers, architects, andtechnicians. One of the most important features of the facility will be its earthquake-resistant design. The hotel will incorporate numerous amenities and services: aconference room, a swimming pool, a beauty salon for all genders, a storecontaining emergency medical support, cycling and touring services, Wi-Fi service, etc. Moreover, this hotel will contain rooms and suites of various types possessing many facilities. Further details about the services and amenities afforded are provided in various sections on our web page.

A unique feature associated with this hotel is the ability of customers to acquire ownership besides regular renting services. Customers will be eligible to purchase ownership starting from just a single share and as far as presidential suites. A single share in this case will be equivalent to 25 square feet. Very importantly, the hotel will be operated and supervised via a certified and efficient workforce in order tooffer the greatest satisfaction for all parties concerned.

Nearest Place

Meghalaya View

You can see the view of the beautiful Meghalaya which is 700m far from our resort. Meghalaya is also known as the wettest place on earth. It is covered in lush forests, pines and bamboos are legion. Various species of birds such as peacocks, pigeons, jungle fowls etc and animals can be spotted and the view is incredible.

Distance: 0.7 km

Tanguar Haor View

Tangor haor is a crucial hub for biodiversity. It is 10-20 minutes away by boat. The place is home to several birds of different origin. Several birds which are migrating can be spotted such as Palace Eagles. Not only that, vultures, seagulls and cranes are also visible and several species of snakes, lizards and turtles can be spotted. A beautiful view which is definitely worthy enough to be visited along with the Meghalayan clouds which create a charming scene.

Distance: 4 km

Lalghat Waterfall View

Sunamganj holds as one of the most beautiful touristic places. It will sound incomplete without the lalghat waterfall view. The waterfall is located at the Sreepur North union. A tribal village is also nearby which is surrounded by several plants and flower garden. You can enjoy the view of Lalghat waterfalls of clear water flowing down the green hills.

Distance: 1 km

Takerghat Niladri Lake View

The nice blue water, the small hillocks and the combination of hills has made Niladri Lake an amazing beauty. Many tourists call it Niladri lake for its distinctive feature, as a Kashmir of Bengal. The place looks other-wordly and is such a serene place.

Distance: 4 km

Barik Tila View

Barek’s Hill is 5 kilometers away from our resort. It is a low lying hill situated in Bangladesh. Emerging from the Meghalaya in India, the Jadukata River flows along the hill. Sand and stones are dredged out of the Jadukata River. The scenic beauty of the Meghalaya, as well as the lively extraction of the the sand and stones are quite enjoyable from the top of the hill. This view will surely hold a special place in the hearts of people enthralled by wanderlust.

Distance: 5 km

Shimul Bagan View

Another view which is quite near our resort is the Shimul Bagan. A field looking so stunning, it will make the whole reality look like a dream-like scenario. A fascinating place to take pictures where the flowers are blooming together in over 2000 trees.

Distance: 7 km
